About Me

I am a detail-oriented and results-driven Data Analyst with a strong academic background in Computer Science and extensive experience in teaching and analytical problem-solving. With over six years of professional experience in the education sector, I have developed a solid foundation in data handling, logical reasoning, and the ability to transform complex information into meaningful insights. My transition into data analytics is driven by a passion for working with data to support business decisions, identify trends, and improve organizational performance. I specialize in tools such as Microsoft Excel, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI, and I continuously enhance my skills through practical projects and real-world datasets.

I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science along with a Bachelor of Education, which has equipped me with both technical expertise and strong communication skills. My professional journey includes teaching Computer Science at the secondary school level, where I was responsible for curriculum planning, student assessment, and integrating technology into education. Additionally, I completed the prestigious MEXT Teacher Training Program in Japan, where I gained international exposure and developed a deeper understanding of modern teaching methodologies and digital tools. This experience strengthened my adaptability, cross-cultural communication, and problem-solving abilities—skills that are highly valuable in data-driven environments.
